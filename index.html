How to Play

1

Rescue Humans

Click on or move to green humans to rescue them. They'll follow you to safety.

2

Lead to Safety

Bring rescued humans to the white safe zone in the top-right corner.

3

Avoid Zombies

Stay away from red zombies! They'll reduce your health if they catch you.

4

Controls

Use mouse clicks or arrow keys to move. You can click anywhere on the map.
